A quick
Intro.

After figuring out what I was doing with my life, I came to the conclusion, do what you most enjoy. So here I am, a second year student at Massey University studying a Bachelor of design, majoring in Visual Communications Design. Doing design work at uni doesn't feel like work at all, it is something I very much enjoy, and tend to do it a lot in my spare time, It is something I'm truely passionate about. My ultimate goal at the end of university is to be a UI/UX designer.

I see myself as a very enthusiastic person who likes to take on new challenges and set the bar high in everything I do. Design isn't all I do though, I am a passionate sailor at the Royal Port Nicolson Yacht Club, competing at multiple match racing events nationally and internationally. I am also a vary keen mountain biker, sending it on any trail I can find.
